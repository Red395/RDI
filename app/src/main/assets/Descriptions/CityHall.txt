17 Church St, Hamilton
City Hall is located in the City of Hamilton and was opened on February 11, 1960. The building was designed by Bermudian Architect Wilfred Onions. He decided to keep Bermuda in mind designing Bermuda with a cottage like theme. On the top of the building is a bronze wind direction indicator resembling the Sea Venture. One of the very first ships to wreck in Bermuda which led to its colonization in 1609. The building today serves many purposes hosting a The Earl Cameron Theatre, the Bermuda National Gallery and the Bermuda Society of Arts.
